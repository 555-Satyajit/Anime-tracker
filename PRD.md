# Anime Companion - Product Requirements Document

## Overview
Not an anime database.
Not an anime streaming site.
A personal hub for anime fans.

## 🏠 Homepage
- **Hero Section**: Trending anime banner, New season announcements, Upcoming episode countdown.
- **Trending Now**: Cards showing Anime image, Rating, Next episode date.
- **Upcoming Episodes**:
  - Tomorrow: One Piece Ep 1137
  - 2 Days: Dandadan Ep 6
  - 4 Days: Kaiju No. 8 Ep 11
- **New Season Announcements**: Solo Leveling S3, JJK S3, Chainsaw Man Movie.
- **Wallpaper Showcase**: Featured wallpapers.

## 👤 Dashboard
When users login.

### My Anime
Shows: Watching, Completed, Plan To Watch, Dropped.
- **Continue Watching**: One Piece (Last watched: 1135, Next episode: 1136).
- **Upcoming For Me**: Only anime user follows.
  - Tomorrow: One Piece
  - Friday: Dandadan
  - Next Month: Chainsaw Man Movie
- **Anime Stats**: Anime Following: 45, Completed: 32, Watching: 8, Episodes Tracked: 3500.

## 🔍 Discover
Search anime.
- **Filters**: Genre, Year, Rating, Studio, Status.

## 🎬 Anime Details Page
- **Overview**: Cover image, Synopsis, Genres, Rating.
- **Episode Tracking**: Current Episode (1135), Next Episode (1136), Release (29 June).
- **Season Status**: Season 2 (Completed), Season 3 (Announced).
- **Trailer**: Embedded video.
- **Related Anime**: Recommendations.
- **Add To My List**: Button.

## 📅 Anime Calendar
Monthly view showing: Episode releases, Movie releases, New seasons. Like Google Calendar.

## 🔔 Notification Center
Users receive: New Episode, New Season, Movie updates.

## 🖼️ Wallpaper Section
Categories: Anime Boys, Anime Girls, Samurai, Dark Fantasy, Cyberpunk, Mythology, Minimalist, Mobile Wallpapers, Desktop Wallpapers.
- **Wallpaper Page**: Preview, Download, Resolution, Tags, Similar wallpapers.
- **User Collections**: Favorite Wallpapers.

## 🤖 AI Section (Later - Phase 3)
Anime Recommendation (Not image generation). User selects Action, Romance, Dark, Comedy and gets recommendations.

## ⚔️ Anime Battles
Weekly polls. Examples: Gojo vs Levi, Naruto vs Luffy.

## 📰 News Section
Only important news. Examples: New season announced, Movie announced, Studio update.

## 🏆 Rankings
Top Anime, Top Movies, Top Characters, Most Followed.

## 📱 Mobile App Feel
Like Spotify, Letterboxd, Crunchyroll. Dark theme, large cover images, smooth animations.
